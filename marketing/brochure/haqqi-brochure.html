  • WPS submission readiness and validation
  • End-of-service gratuity calculations with transparent logic
  • Termination settlement workflows and downloadable summaries
  • Documented records for internal review and external audits

The Problem We Solve

Many SMBs in the GCC manage payroll compliance and employee settlements through disconnected spreadsheets, calculators, and manual approvals. This creates risk at exactly the moments when accuracy matters most.

Payroll deadlinesTeams rush to validate data before WPS submissions.
Employee exitsFinance and HR reconcile gratuity and notice pay manually.
Audits or disputesSupporting documents are scattered across files and inboxes.
